Instructions:
Destroy all enemies before time runs out to win. If your health reaches 0 or time runs out you lose.
Use WASD keys with traditional "tank controls", left and right arrow keys to rotate the turret, Spacebar to fire, Esc to pause. 
Wrench & Screwdriver powerup increases health by 1 (no upper limit on health). Arrow powerup increases speed for a short time. Lightning powerup increases fire rate for a short time.

Game has autosave feature. After a level is completed, it saves your progress automatically. Press left-alt + D and relaunch game to delete save data.
